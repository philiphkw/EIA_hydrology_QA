9.1 Introduction
In accordance with the provisions of Law No. 19,300 on General Bases of the Environment, and its
modifications, and in the D.S. No. 40/2012 of the MMA, and its modifications, Regulations of the System of
Environmental Impact Assessment, specifically in its articles 18, letter k), and article 105, in this
Chapter presents the Monitoring Plan for the environmental variables of the “Burn Extraction Project”.
Clays for the Production of Rare Earth Concentrates”.
This originates according to the results of Chapters 4, 5 and 7 of this EIA, since the Project will generate
or present some effects, characteristics or circumstances referred to in article 11 of the Law
19,300, which make it necessary to implement
n of various follow-up or monitoring measures
environmental.
In accordance with what is indicated in article 105 of the aforementioned D.S. N°40/2012, this plan aims to
ensure that all relevant environmental variables that were subject to environmental assessment
evolve as projected.
The Environmental Monitoring Plan has been prepared in card format and structured considering the
following contents:
• Project phase
• Component of the environment that will be subject to measurement and control
• Environmental impact and associated measurement
• Location of control points
• Parameters that will be used to characterize the state and evolution of said component
• Allowed or committed limits
• Duration and frequency of the monitoring plan for each parameter
• Measurement method or procedure for each parameter
• Time and frequency of delivery of reports with the evaluation of the results
• Other relevant aspects

9.2 Objectives
9.2.1 General Objective
The purpose of this plan is to define actions and activities that will need to be carried out, in order to
verify the effectiveness of the measures proposed in the Plan of Mitigation, Repair and/or Measures
Compensation (Chapter 7 of the EIA). Thus, through its implementation it will be possible to verify that the factors
environmental issues with relevance to the Project evolve as forecast.
9.2.2 Specific Objectives
• Ensure that the relevant environmental variables that were the subject of environmental evaluation
evolve as projected.
• Verify the effectiveness of the proposed mitigation, repair and/or compensation measures
for those significant impacts.
• For the variables that will be subject to monitoring, detect early any
unforeseen and unwanted effect, so that it is possible to control it by timely applying
the relevant measures or actions.
9.3 Methodology
The Environmental Monitoring Plan incorporates the monitoring of the environmental variables that gave rise to
to the EIA in accordance with art. 11 of Law 19,300, as well as the relevant environmental variables that were subject
of environmental evaluation that are estimated to need to be observed during all phases of the Project.
By virtue of what is indicated in article 105 of the SEIA Regulations, the Plan has been prepared in accordance
in accordance with the general instructions issued by the Superintendence of the Environment and
contains, when appropriate, for each phase of the Project, the environmental component that
will be subject to measurement and control; the environmental impact and the associated measure; the location of the points
control; the parameters that will be used to characterize the state and evolution of said
component; the limits allowed or committed; the duration and frequency of the monitoring plan
for each parameter; the method or procedure for measuring each parameter; the term and frequency
delivery of reports with the evaluation of the results and any other aspect that has been estimated
relevant.
For the design of the monitoring plan, the instructions of the SMA have been taken into consideration.
contained in Res Ex N°223 of March 26, 2015, and its modifications, which dictates instructions
generalities regarding the preparation of a Monitoring Plan for Environmental Variables.
