DIAGNOSIS
STATISTICAL ANALYSIS: CONCLUSION
Relevant topics
is:
• Of the 46,176 inhabitants in Penco, 10,070 are registered as dependent workers.
• Unemployment in Penco has increased by 7% in the second quarter of 2023
• Municipal budget availability is lower than nearby communes.
• Few green public areas and greater distances compared to neighboring communities
• Low educational and schooling levels
• There is a significant digital divide in the region

DIAGNOSIS
LOCAL ANALYSIS → PLADECO AND POLICIES: CONCLUSION
Relevant topics:
• Comprehensive Approach: The municipality focuses on several aspects, including administration,
entrepreneurship, heritage protection, public safety and citizen participation.
• Simplification and Modernization: Priority is given to simplifying procedures and modernizing
municipal facilities.
• Local Development and Tourism: The municipality seeks to promote local economic development and promote
Penco as a tourist destination.
• Sustainability and Health: Attention is paid to sustainability, waste management, mobility
sustainable and health promotion.
• Inclusion and Equity: The inclusion of marginalized communities is promoted and seeks to reduce
territorial inequality in access to social benefits.
