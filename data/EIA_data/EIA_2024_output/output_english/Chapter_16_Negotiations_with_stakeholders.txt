16.1 Introduction
Article 17 of Supreme Decree No. 40, of 2012, of the MMA, and its modifications, System Regulations
of Environmental Impact Assessment (RSEIA), points out that: “The proponents must inform the
environmental authority if they have established, before entering the evaluation process, negotiations with the
interested parties in order to agree on environmental compensation or mitigation measures, individualizing
in such case to the people with whom the negotiation was established, as well as the content and result of
this one. The same obligation will apply if said negotiations are carried out during the evaluation procedure.
environmental, case in which the proponent will report directly to the Regional Director or the Director
Service Executive, as appropriate. If such agreements exist, they will not be binding.
for the environmental qualification of the project or activity.”
