13.2.6 Participatory monitoring
Article 2 literal g bis of the D.S. N°40/2012 of the MMA, Regulation of the Evaluation System of
Environmental Impact (RSEIA), and its modification D.S. N°30/2023, establishes the following:
Participatory monitoring: “Process through which the owner incorporates the community into the
monitoring the phases of the development of a project through the delivery of information, reports,
measurements, carrying out training, coordination of field visits or others that account for
of the development of the project or activity, in any of its phases.”
Likewise, article 18 letter m bis) of the same regulation indicates that:
“The description of the participatory monitoring that the project or activity incorporates for the
monitoring the phases of its development. The place and time in which they will be verified must be indicated,
as well as compliance indicators.”
Next, the CAV associated with participatory monitoring is presented.
