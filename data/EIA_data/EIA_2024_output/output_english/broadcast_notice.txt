In accordance with the provisions of Article 87 of the Regulations of the Impact Assessment System
Environmental, D.S. Nº40/2012, of the Ministry of the Environment, and its modifications, the Project Owner
will make five radio announcements, between 9:00 a.m. and 9:00 p.m., on different days, within 5 business days
after the last publication of the respective extract, in a local broadcasting medium of
the commune of the area of influence of the Project. The proposed text of the notice, which will be broadcast on the radio,
is the following:
Proposed notice text:
REE Uno SpA. informs the community that it has submitted to the Environmental Impact Assessment System the
Environmental Impact Study of its “Mining Development Project for Clay Extraction for Production
of Rare Earth Concentrates”. In summary, the Project considers three clay extraction zones
(named Victoria Norte, Luna and Maite), a disposal area for ballast and washed clays
(called Neptune), a topsoil collection area, a clay processing plant, a
stockpile for the temporary collection of material prior to processing, an electrical line and its connection to
existing line and the construction of internal roads to access the different areas. The Plant
Processing will have a capacity to process up to 320 tons/hour of material to produce
Rare Earth concentrate. In addition, the modification of an existing section of
approximately 1.22 kilometers of an oil pipeline owned by ENAP.
The project will be located in the Biobío Region, province of Biobío, communes of Penco and Concepción.
For the information of citizens, the Environmental Impact Study is available for consultation
on the website of the Environmental Evaluation Service, www.sea.gob.cl and access to the physical document in the
offices of the Biobío Region Environmental Assessment Service, located at 145 Lincoyán Street Concepción, the
which can be consulted during office hours, on business days from Monday to Friday, from 8:30 a.m. to 1:30 p.m.; in
the Municipality of Penco, located at 500 Penco O'Higgins Street, on business days from Monday to Friday from 8:30 a.m.
1:30 p.m.; in the Municipality of Concepción, located at Bernardo O`Higgins Street 525 Concepción, in days

442 Aníbal Pinto Street, Second Floor Concepción, from Monday to Friday from 8:30 a.m. to 1:30 p.m.; and in the
Regional Government of Biobío, located at Avenida Arturo Prat 525 Concepción, from Monday to Friday
from 8:30 a.m. to 1:00 p.m.
